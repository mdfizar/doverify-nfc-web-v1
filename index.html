Where Are Some Frequently Asked Questions

What is doverify NFC Smart Card?

Answer: Doverify NFC Smart Card is a modern digital business card that uses NFC technology to share contact info wirelessly and instantly. Just tap your Doverify card with your phone or scan the QR Code to instantly access all the details.

Why choose the Doverify NFC Smart Card?

- Unique Manageable Portal: You can change information anytime, anywhere.
- Lifetime Usage: One card for a lifetime with no additional costs.
- Job Changes: Easily update details even when changing jobs through the portal.
- Advanced Features: Includes unique and advanced functionalities for enhanced usability.
- Environmentally friendly: Reduces the need for printed business cards, promoting sustainability.

What are the advantages of using an NFC smart business card?

Answer: Convenience: Easy sharing of contact information with a simple tap. Versatility: Can store more than just basic contact details, such as links to social media profiles or portfolios. Environmentally friendly: Reduces the need for printed business cards, promoting sustainability. Modern and innovative: Leaves a lasting impression on recipients who appreciate tech-savvy solutions.

Are NFC smart business cards compatible with all smartphones?

Answer: Most modern smartphones support NFC technology. However, it's advisable to check if your recipient's phone has NFC capabilities before relying solely on NFC cards for distribution.

Can I customize the information stored on an NFC smart business card?

Answer: Yes, you can customize the information stored on the NFC chip. This typically includes your name, job title, company name, phone number, email address, website URL, and any other relevant details you wish to share.

How secure are NFC smart business cards?

Answer: NFC smart business cards are designed to transfer information securely. They do not require an internet connection to work, reducing the risk of interception or hacking during the data transfer process. However, like any digital technology, it's essential to exercise caution when sharing sensitive information.

How do I create or obtain an NFC smart business card?

Answer: Create your own: Purchase NFC-enabled cards and program them with your information using a compatible smartphone or NFC writer. Order from providers: Many companies specialize in creating NFC smart business cards. They often offer customization options and can preload your information onto the cards.
